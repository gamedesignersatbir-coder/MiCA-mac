

Programme overview
The Art of Living Happiness Programme is a 3-day experiential workshop that teaches practical tools to manage stress, calm the mind, and live with more energy and joy. Through a blend of yoga, powerful breathing techniques (including Sudarshan Kriya), guided meditation, and simple life wisdom, participants learn how to handle everyday challenges with greater ease and positivity.

Key components
Sudarshan Kriya (SKY Breath): A unique, research-backed rhythmic breathing technique that helps release deep-rooted stress, negative emotions and fatigue, and brings the mind to a clear, relaxed yet alert state.

Yogic breathing and pranayama: A series of simple breathing exercises that quickly calm the nervous system, increase energy, and improve emotional balance.

Gentle yoga and guided meditations: Easy physical stretches and meditations suitable for all fitness levels to relax the body and settle the mind.

Practical wisdom and processes: Interactive activities and discussions that build self-awareness, improve relationships, and offer practical ways to live with more happiness in daily life.
Core benefits and outcomes
Significant reduction in stress, anxiety, and tension; participants report feeling calmer, lighter, and more centered from the very first sessions.

Increased daily energy levels, improved quality of sleep, and better ability to focus and manage workload.

Greater emotional resilience and improved mood, including reductions in symptoms of depression and anger, and more optimism and enthusiasm.

Stronger relationships and communication, as participants learn to respond rather than react, and connect more positively with family, friends and colleagues.
